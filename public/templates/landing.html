What is ViRT?

Virt is an app that lets you find something to do when you're in a new neighborhood or you aren't sure what's going on at this moment. You can search according to your preferences in taste, distance, and whether or not you're into paying a small cover or just want something free to see.

The Business Owners

Small venues, clubs (heck, even restaurants!) can increase their attendance and crowds by creating their free ViRT accounts, posting a quick blurb about their unique event and it will appear in the search results for people nearby looking to check something cool out.
